Our Journey

Proudly Serving Northampton Since 2002

At Eastern Balti, we are more than just a restaurant; we are a timeless story. With over two decades of dedication to Northampton, our roots run deep, and our culinary heritage is stronger than ever.

Our journey blends Indian tradition with a contemporary twist, resulting in a vibrant and diverse menu bursting with authentic flavors created from the finest, locally-sourced ingredients.

Join us on a thrilling culinary adventure that marries tradition and innovation, celebrating the very best of India's rich and diverse cuisine.

  • Exquisite Chicken or Meat Tikka Shatkora

    £7.50

    Embark on a journey of flavors with our Chicken or Meat Tikka Shatkora – where tender tikka dances with the tangy allure of Sylheti citrus in a rich and flavorful sauce. It's a taste adventure that leaves a lasting impression.

  • Signature House Special Curry

    £8.50

    Delight in our Signature House Special Curry: an exquisite harmony of succulent chicken, flavorful meat, plump prawns, and vibrant veggies, all bathed in a rich medium sauce. Served with fragrant pilau rice, it's a culinary masterpiece crafted for your delight.

  • Royal King Prawn Dhanya

    £8.50

    Indulge in the succulence of our Royal King Prawn Dhanya: plump king prawns bathed in a special and aromatic sauce, garnished with the vibrant freshness of coriander. It's a regal treat for your senses.

  • Regal Raj Rani Murgi

    £7.50
